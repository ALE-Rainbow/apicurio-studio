    What is this page?

    Hey there! Here you can import an existing API to the Studio by telling us where the API's OpenAPI document is located.

    Simply provide the URL of your OpenAPI document and we'll do the rest! The URL can point directly to the content, or it can be a source control URL (GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket).

    What do I do?

    Simply copy/paste (or drag and drop!) the URL of your existing OpenAPI document from your browser into the form and then click "Import API". Easy peasy, lemon squeezy!
